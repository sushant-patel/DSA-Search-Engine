 CSES - Giant Pizza      Uolevi's family is going to order a large pizza and eat it together. A total of nn family members will join the order, and there are mm possible toppings. The pizza may have any number of toppings.  Each family member gives two wishes concerning the toppings of the pizza. The wishes are of the form "topping x is good/bad". Your task is to choose the toppings so that at least one wish from everybody becomes true (a good topping is included in the pizza or a bad topping is not included).
