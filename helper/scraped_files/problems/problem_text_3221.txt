 CSES - Movie Festival Queries      In a movie festival, nn movies will be shown. You know the starting and ending time of each movie.  Your task is to process qq queries of the form: if you arrive and leave the festival at specific times, what is the maximum number of movies you can watch?  You can watch two movies if the first movie ends before or exactly when the second movie starts. You can start the first movie exactly when you arrive and leave exactly when the last movie ends.
