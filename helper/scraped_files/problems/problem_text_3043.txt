 CSES - Coin Combinations II      Consider a money system consisting of nn coins. Each coin has a positive integer value. Your task is to calculate the number of distinct ordered ways you can produce a money sum xx using the available coins.  For example, if the coins are {2,3,5} and the desired sum is 9, there are 3 ways:  2+2+5  3+3+3  2+2+2+3
