 CSES - Forbidden Cities      There are nn cities and mm roads between them. Kaaleppi is currently in city a and wants to travel to city b.  However, there is a problem: Kaaleppi has recently robbed a bank in city c and can't enter the city, because the local police would catch him. Your task is to find out if there is a route from city a to city b that does not visit city c.  As an additional challenge, you have to process q queries where a, b and c vary.
