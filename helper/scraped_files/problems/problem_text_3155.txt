 CSES - Stick Game      Consider a game where two players remove sticks from a heap. The players move alternately, and the player who removes the last stick wins the game.  A set P={p1,p2,…,pk}P={p1,p2,…,pk} determines the allowed moves. For example, if P={1,3,4}P={1,3,4}, a player may remove 1, 3 or 4 sticks.  Your task is find out for each number of sticks 1,2,…,n if the first player has a winning or losing position.
