 CSES - Houses and Schools      There are nn houses on a street, numbered 1,2,…,n1,2,…,n. The distance of houses a and b is |a−b|. You know the number of children in each house.  Your task is to establish k schools in such a way that each school is in some house. Then, each child goes to the nearest school. What is the minimum total walking distance of the children if you act optimally?
